Pool Renovations & Remodeling

BREATHE NEW LIFE
INTO YOUR BACKYARD.

Whether your pool is suffering from cracked plaster or you're simply ready for a massive aesthetic upgrade, our renovation teams specialize in structural and cosmetic overhauls.

Transform Your Existing Canvas

A renovation isn't just a repair; it's an opportunity to modernize your entire outdoor living experience and increase your property value.

Resurfacing & Plaster

Say goodbye to rough, cracked, or stained surfaces. We strip down to the gunite and apply premium exposed-aggregate pebble finishes or silky smooth quartz for a brilliant, durable interior.

Tile & Coping Replacement

Update the perimeter with stunning glass mosaic tiles, natural stone, or modern porcelain. Paired with fresh travertine or limestone coping, it instantly modernizes your pool's profile.

Structural Additions

Want to add a spillover spa where there isn't one? Or maybe a large sun shelf (baja ledge) for lounging? We have the engineering expertise to modify the structural shape of your existing pool.

Equipment Overhauls

Rip out that noisy, inefficient pump and ancient heater. We swap in whisper-quiet variable-speed pumps, saltwater chlorinators, and highly efficient heat pumps to slash your energy bills.

The ROI of a Remodel.

An outdated pool can be an eyesore and an energy hog. By upgrading your surface, tile, and equipment, you not only elevate your daily enjoyment but drastically reduce your monthly maintenance and electrical costs.
