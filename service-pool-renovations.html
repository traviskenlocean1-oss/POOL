Pool Renovations & Remodeling

BREATHE NEW LIFE
INTO YOUR BACKYARD.

Your aging pool doesn't mean starting over. We handle everything from cosmetic resurfacing to full structural rebuilds.

What We Can Transform

Transform Your
Existing Canvas

A renovation isn't just a repair — it's an opportunity to modernize your entire outdoor experience and increase your property value.

Resurfacing & Plaster

Say goodbye to rough, cracked, or stained surfaces. We strip down to the gunite shell and apply premium exposed-aggregate pebble or silky smooth quartz finishes for a brilliant, durable interior.

Tile & Coping Replacement

Update the perimeter with stunning glass mosaic tiles, natural stone, or modern porcelain. Fresh travertine or limestone coping instantly modernizes your pool's profile and adds lasting resale value.

Structural Additions

Want to add a spa where there isn't one, or a large sun shelf for lounging? We have the engineering expertise to modify the structural shape of your existing pool shell entirely.

Equipment Overhauls

Rip out that noisy, inefficient old pump. We swap in whisper-quiet variable-speed pumps, saltwater chlorinators, and high-efficiency heat pumps to slash your monthly energy bills.
