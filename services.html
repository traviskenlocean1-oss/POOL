Custom Pool Design & Build

From the first sketch to the day you swim, we manage every phase. Our design team builds 3D renderings of your pool before a single shovel breaks ground — so you can see the exact shape, depth, tile, and finish before we start. No surprises. No rework.

  • In-home design consultation & 3D rendering
  • Site assessment, grading & permit management
  • Structural excavation & gunite/shotcrete shell
  • Full plumbing, electrical & bonding
  • Tile, coping & finish selection + installation
  • Equipment installation & startup walkthrough

Pool Renovations & Remodeling

An aging pool doesn't mean starting over. We handle full-scale renovations — from surface resurfacing to structural rebuilds. Cracked plaster, outdated coping, broken equipment — we bring pools back to like-new condition, often for a fraction of replacement cost.

  • Plaster, pebble & quartz resurfacing
  • Tile replacement, coping & waterline upgrades
  • Structural crack repair & shell restoration
  • Deck resurfacing & paver replacement
  • Equipment upgrades: pumps, filters, heaters
  • New plumbing, LED lighting & bonding

Water Features & Spa Integration

The right feature transforms a pool into an experience. Cascading waterfalls, grottos, sun shelves, sheer descents, fire bowls, and spillover spas — we design and build features that add drama, ambiance, and real resale value to your outdoor space.

  • Natural rock & stacked-stone waterfalls
  • Sheer descents, rain curtains & deck jets
  • Integrated spas, hot tubs & spillover systems
  • Sun shelves, tanning ledges & bubblers
  • Fire bowls, fire features & gas line work
  • Color-changing LED lighting packages
