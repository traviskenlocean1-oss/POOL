Custom Pool Design & Build

FROM BARE EARTH
TO PARADISE.

We design and construct master-crafted swimming pools, managing every phase of the build from the first 3D sketch to the final equipment startup.

No Surprises. No Rework.

Building a custom pool is a massive investment. We believe in absolute transparency and flawless execution, which is why we break our process down into highly managed phases.

01

3D Modeling & Approval

Before a single shovel hits the dirt, we present a photorealistic 3D render of your new backyard. You will see the exact tile, water color, decking materials, and layout, ensuring your complete alignment with the vision.

02

Permitting & Excavation

We handle all HOA approvals, city permits, and structural engineering documents. Once cleared, our heavy-equipment team conducts precise excavation and builds the robust steel framework that gives your pool its lifetime structural integrity.

03

Gunite, Plumbing & Finishes

After shooting the dense gunite shell, we install high-flow plumbing systems built for efficiency. Then comes the artistry: setting waterline tile, pouring custom coping, and applying premium quartz or pebble interiors.

The Aqualine Guarantee

A beautifully designed pool is only as good as the engineering beneath it. We over-engineer every shell, oversize our plumbing to reduce energy strain, and only install equipment we trust in our own homes.

  • Lifetime structural shell warranty
  • 3-Year comprehensive equipment warranty
  • Dedicated project manager assigned to you
