Our Build Process

No Surprises.
No Rework.

Building a custom pool is a massive investment. We believe in absolute transparency and flawless execution across every highly-managed phase.

01

3D Modeling & Design Approval

Before a single shovel hits the dirt, you'll see a photorealistic 3D render of your new backyard. The exact tile, water color, decking material, and layout — fully visualized and approved by you before we mobilize.

02

Permitting & Precision Excavation

We handle every HOA approval, city permit, and structural engineering document. Then our heavy equipment team performs precision excavation and builds the robust steel rebar framework that forms the permanent structural backbone of your pool.

03

Gunite Shell, Plumbing & Premium Finishes

After shooting the dense gunite shell, we install high-flow plumbing engineered for efficiency and longevity. Then comes the artistry — setting waterline tile, pouring custom coping, and applying premium pebble or quartz interior finishes.

The Aqualine Guarantee

A beautiful pool is only as good as the engineering beneath it. We over-engineer every shell, oversize our plumbing to reduce energy strain, and only install equipment we would trust in our own homes.
