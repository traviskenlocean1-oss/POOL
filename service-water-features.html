Water Features & Spa Integrations

BRING YOUR POOL TO LIFE.

The right feature transforms a static pool into a sensory experience. Enhance your backyard with the sights and sounds of cascading water and dramatic fire bowls.

Waterfalls & Grottos

There's nothing quite like the soothing sound of moving water. We design everything from sleek, modern sheer descents that sheet water seamlessly from elevated walls, to massive, natural stone cascading grottos that you can swim into.

  • Natural stacked stone cascades
  • Sheer descents & rain curtains
  • Custom swim-in grottos

Integrated Spas & Fire

A raised spillover spa creates a stunning visual focal point while offering year-round therapeutic relaxation. Pair that with automated gas fire bowls resting on masonry columns, and your pool becomes the ultimate nighttime retreat.

  • Raised & flush spillover spas
  • Automated gas fire bowls
  • High-output therapy jets
