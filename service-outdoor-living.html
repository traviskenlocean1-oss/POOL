Your pool is the centerpiece, but the surrounding space dictates how you truly live in it. We handle every detail — from decking to shade to fire.

Custom Decking & Pavers

From cool-touch tumbled travertine and natural stone to sleek porcelain pavers — we install premium decking that resists Florida's intense heat and beautifully frames your pool's geometry.

Pergolas & Shade Structures

Beat the South Florida sun with architectural pergolas, pavilions, and cabanas. We offer custom wood builds, modern aluminum structures, and automated louvered roof systems.

Fire Pits & Features

Extend your evenings with custom gas fire pits, raised fire bowls, and linear outdoor fireplaces integrated directly into your new deck — keeping guests lingering long after sunset.

Summer Kitchens

Fully equipped outdoor cooking spaces featuring built-in gas grills, beverage centers, weatherproof cabinetry, and custom stone countertops — built to handle the Florida climate beautifully.
