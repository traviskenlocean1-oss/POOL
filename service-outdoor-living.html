Outdoor Living & Decking

BEYOND THE WATER.

A great pool deserves an equally breathtaking surround. We design and build comprehensive outdoor living spaces that turn your backyard into a luxury resort.

Complete Backyard Transformations

Your pool is the centerpiece, but the surrounding space dictates how you live in it. We handle every detail of your outdoor expansion.

Custom Decking & Pavers

From cool-touch tumbled travertine and natural stone to sleek porcelain pavers, we install premium decking that resists heat and enhances your pool's geometry.

Pergolas & Shade Structures

Beat the Florida sun with architectural pergolas, pavilions, and cabanas. We offer custom wood builds, modern aluminum structures, and louvered roof systems.

Fire Pits & Features

Extend your swimming season into the evening with custom gas fire pits, raised fire bowls, and linear outdoor fireplaces integrated directly into your deck.

Summer Kitchens

Fully equipped outdoor cooking spaces featuring built-in gas grills, beverage centers, weatherproof cabinetry, and custom stone countertops.

One Unified Vision.

Avoid the headache of hiring separate contractors for your pool, deck, and patio roof. Because we design and build the entire environment, every material, elevation, and drainage system is perfectly synchronized.
